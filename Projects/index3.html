Websites for Change Project

This project is a website that works to inform people about the inequalities children with disabilities face, especially when it comes to learning how to ride a bicycle. The website provides an opportunity for readers to learn more about this cause and what local organizations are doing to support this issue.

Choose Your Own Adventure Game

This Concert Simulator game is a fun, interactive game to simulate the experiences and choices someone may have to make when attending a concert. The player's choices throughout the concert will ultimately determine their fate at the end of their concert experience.
