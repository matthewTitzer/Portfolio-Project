My Extracurriculars



Cyclebar Spin Classes

Evansville's Cyclebar Studio allows me to get high-intensity workouts on stationary bikes. Upbeat music, an encouraging instructor, and a class of other individuals motivated to exercise allow me to improve my physical health and strengthen my passion for cycling.

Mock Trial

With Signature School's Mock Trial Team, I learn how our legal system works by acting out sample court cases provided by the Indiana Bar Foundation. I work with other students to develop both a case theory and the characters of each witness.

Jazz Band

In Signature School's Jazz Band, I am a member of the percussion section and get the opportunity to play various rhythm instruments. I am able to express my creativity in playing music and incorporate music into my everyday life.

Golf/Tennis/Pickleball

Throughout my life, I have learned how to play golf, tennis, and pickleball and continue to practice these sports to improve my physical health and increase my social activity with my family and friends.
